# Support for Wildcard

Need help with Wildcard? We're here for you.

## Contact

Email us at [egoshin.georgii@gmail.com](mailto:egoshin.georgii@gmail.com).

We read every message and usually reply within a few business days.

## What to include

To help us resolve your issue faster, please mention:

- Your iOS version
- The Wildcard app version (Settings → bottom of the screen)
- A short description of what happened and what you expected
